CONTROL ROOM


Welcome aboard this anonymous deep sea research vessel. A couple housekeeping rules, and you can be on your way.

It might take you a while to find your sea legs, but maps in the STAIRWELLS and in NAVIGATION will help you find your way. Feel free to explore the ship and see the kinds of things we get up to around here. Our captain is always looking to add little bits and bobs around the place.

I'd recommend COMMUNICATION, where you can broadcast whatever you please into the open sea, and the OBSERVATION DECK, where you can look through our periscope and see what anyone else has broadcasted.

Use the bottom bar to walk through the submarine!
